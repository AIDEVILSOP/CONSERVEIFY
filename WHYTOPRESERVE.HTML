Sustain, Don't Let It Drain

conserveify.com

Energy conservation is when you make a conscious decision to use less energy.

You might be wondering, can it really be that simple? Yes, yes, it can! Not only is it simple, but it’s also quite beneficial.

When we lower the amount of energy we use, we slow down fossil fuel depletion and help clean up the environment. While we’re at it, we end up saving ourselves money.

Let’s take a closer look.

WHY SOULD WE PRESERVE ENERGY??

Directly or indirectly reinvigorates the way of living
Uplifts the economic conditions of a country
Acts as a barrier for climate change on a global scale
Improve air quality and reduce airborne allergens
Save coastal cities from disappearing underwater
Delay fossil fuel price increases
Includes measures for management and conservation of wildlife habitats
Sustainable development goal 7 and 11 emphasize on energy conservation
Addresses the socio economic dimensions of socities for mutual benefit of people
A clean future and better environment for your kids
Transportation and commerce may grind to a halt
Accords researchers more time to formulate solutions and alternatives
