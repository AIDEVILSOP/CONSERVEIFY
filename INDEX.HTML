ENERGY NEWS

Accelerated Power Systems

At the Clean Energy Ministerial, the UNEP announced grant support to four pilot projects in Brazil, Colombia, India, and Morocco.

The project will support these countries in accelerating progress on power system modernisation and effective utilization of distributed energy resources through proper guidance.

Energy Efficient Solutions

United for Energy Efficiency (U4E) is a global effort supporting developing countries to move their markets to energy-efficient appliances.

U4E brings together key stakeholders in the area of product efficiency to inform policymakers, promote global best practices and provide tailored assistance to governments.

Non Fossil Fuels

A bill seeking to mandate the use of non-fossil energy sources such as biomass, ethanol and green hydrogen was passed by the Lok Sabha

It also seeks to introduce new concepts such as carbon trading and mandate the use of non-fossil sources to ensure faster decarbonisation of the Indian economy

Energy Conservation Building

In a bid to promote energy conservation, the Punjab Government has decided to construct a super Energy Conservation Building Code (ECBC) compliant building in Mohali.

Energy Sources Minister said that 100 sq m land has been allocated to PEDA for the construction of the super ECBC building in Sector 65.
